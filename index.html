About Me

Hello, I'm Sebastian Price. I have been coding since I was 18 years old and have finally created a space to post my projects, ideas and other things that I find interesting.

I am from Atlanta, GA and a graduate of Georgia Southern University with a Bachelor's in Computer Science. After graduation I moved to Orlando, Fl to start my career as a Programmer Analyst with Aon Hewitt. After 5 years with Aon, I decided to leave the firm and enroll in DigitalCrafts 16 week Immersive Coding bootcamp to further my programming knowledge and become more skilled in web programming and software development.
